Hello!

I'm a Chinese-Indonesian UX/UI designer based in Brooklyn, NY. I'm passionate about creating design concepts and solutions that empower users to foster community and engage in their experiences. I'm a firm believer that design is a gift that allows you to pay it forward by shaping human experiences into a foundation for building community.

My background is one of diversity, where a merging of different stories, experiences, and vibrant cultures were a norm. Being raised in an immigrant family taught me the power of community from an early age. When I reflect back on my fondest memories, they often manifest in little anecdotes with a common theme of sustaining and nurturing human connection.

Though my journey into design was not a straight-forward one, I prefer to think of it as a series of happy coincidences that brought me to where I am today. I strive to bridge my natural curiosity and empathy towards society with my background in research and analysis, to design innovative solutions with the needs of communities and people at the core.

These days, I'm on a journey to become the best at what I do. Along the way, I hope to continue creating and sharing more stories. (Preferably over pizza, or some really good tunes!)

If you share a similar enthusiasm, or are looking to add more of this to your team, feel free to say hello. I'm always up for a good conversation!
